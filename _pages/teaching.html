--- layout: archive title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true --- ## Teaching I approach teaching political science as the cultivation of responsible, analytical citizenship. Across my courses, I emphasize critical thinking, evidence evaluation, respectful debate, and the development of skills that extend beyond the classroom. My teaching style combines high expectations with structured support, encouraging students to challenge themselves while providing the tools necessary for success. Through discussion-based learning, scaffolded assignments, and active engagement opportunities, I seek to create classrooms that are rigorous, yet approachable. Student feedback frequently highlights my clarity, accessibility, and investment in student growth. Across multiple courses, students have emphasized my ability to make complex political concepts engaging and approachable while maintaining meaningful academic standards.
